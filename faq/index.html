Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our legal services and process.

What does RLC bring to its clientele?

We focus on being a Strategic Legal Counsel for Growth-Focused Businesses by providing and delivering practical, business-oriented legal solutions to banks, international investors, and startups operating in Kenya’s dynamic commercial environment.

Our team has deep expertise in civil & commercial litigation, banking and securities, cross-border transactions, regulatory compliance, and corporate structuring enabling us to support both complex deals and early-stage growth.

Do you offer legal retainers for companies or SMEs?

Yes. We provide tailored legal retainer packages that give your business access to ongoing legal support for corporate governance, contract reviews, legal compliance, and employment matters without the cost of in-house counsel.

Do I need an appointment before visiting?

Yes, we recommend booking an appointment to ensure that an advocate is available to meet with you. You can schedule by:

  • Phone: 0207 800 800
  • Email: office@rlcadvocates.co.ke

Do you offer initial consultations?

Yes. We offer an initial consultation to assess your matter and advise on the way forward. This may be offered at no charge or a nominal fee, depending on the nature of the matter.

What documents should I bring to my first consultation?

Depending on your issue, please bring:

  • Title deeds or agreements (for conveyancing)
  • Loan or security documents
  • Contracts, correspondence or court documents
  • Company registration documents

How do you charge for legal services?

Our fees are guided by the Advocates Remuneration Order. We provide:

  • Clear fee estimates after an initial consultation
  • Flexible billing options depending on complexity.
  • Transparent invoicing throughout your matter.

Do you offer fixed fees or payment plans?

For certain transactions such as conveyancing or contract drafting, we may offer fixed or capped fees. Payment plans can be arranged for corporate or ongoing matters on request.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes. We serve clients locally and internationally via Zoom, Google Meet, Phone lines, and Email. We handle many transactions and legal matters remotely with the same level of attention and professionalism.

Do you handle matters outside Nairobi or internationally?

Yes. We work with clients across Kenya and if need be, we collaborate with regional and international firms where cross-border issues arise. Our services extend to foreign investors and diaspora clients as well.

Can you help with due diligence?

Absolutely. We conduct due diligence for:

  • Real estate transactions (ownership, charges, encumbrances)
  • Corporate acquisitions, mergers or investments

This protects our clients from legal and financial risks.

Do you assist with drafting and reviewing contracts?

Yes. We draft, review and negotiate contracts including shareholder agreements, sale or lease agreements, Joint ventures, MOUs, loan, and service agreements, ensuring they are legally sound.

What if I receive a demand letter or court summons?

Contact us immediately. Legal notices often have strict deadlines. We’ll assess the documents, advise on your rights, and respond appropriately whether through negotiation or court representation.

How do you protect client confidentiality?

We are bound by the Advocates Act and Data Protection Act. All client information and documents are handled with strict confidentiality, both during and after our engagement.

How do I engage your services?

To get started, simply call us, send an email to office@rlcadvocates.co.ke, or schedule a consultation via our website. Our team will respond within 24 hours.

RLC Advocates LLP is a premier legal services firm offering top-tier consultancy.
