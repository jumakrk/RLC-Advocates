1. Introduction and Scope

At RLC Advocates, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our clients' and visitors' information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you engage with our legal services, visit our website, or interact with our firm.

Our commitment to legal professional privilege and attorney-client confidentiality remains our highest priority. This policy outlines our practices in compliance with global data protection standards, including GDPR and local legal frameworks.

2. Information We Collect

In the course of providing legal representation and advice, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Identity Data: Full name, professional title, and identification documents for KYC purposes.
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number, and physical office or residential addresses.
  • Case Data: Information related to your legal matters, including sensitive information required for litigation or advisory services.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage patterns on our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

Legal Services

To perform the contract we are about to enter into or have entered into with you regarding legal representation.

Communication

To send you case updates, legal insights, and administrative information regarding your engagement with the firm.

4. Data Security and Storage

We implement industry-standard security measures, including end-to-end encryption for digital files and secure off-site archiving for physical documents. Access to your personal data is strictly limited to authorized legal professionals and staff who have a "need to know" basis for providing our services.

security_update_good

Advanced Encryption

All digital communications and client portal data are protected by 256-bit AES encryption and regular vulnerability assessments.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have specific rights regarding your personal information:

check_circle Right to access your personal data and receive a copy.
check_circle Right to rectify inaccurate or incomplete information.
check_circle Right to request erasure of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
check_circle Right to object to or restrict processing for marketing purposes.

Contact Our Privacy Officer

If you have questions about this policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact our compliance team directly.
