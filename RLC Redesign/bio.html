Professional Biography

With over 25 years of experience in high-end corporate litigation and mergers, Alexander Sterling has established himself as a preeminent authority in the global legal landscape. As the Senior Partner at RLC Advocates, he spearheads the Corporate and Securities practice, representing a diverse clientele ranging from tech unicorns to multinational conglomerates.

Before joining RLC, Alexander served as Chief Legal Counsel for an international investment firm, giving him a unique dual perspective on both business objectives and legal risk management. His philosophy centers on proactive, strategic architecture—building legal frameworks that not only protect assets but catalyze growth.
