Protecting Your Creative Assets

Sep 15, 2025 | By RLC Advocates

In an information-driven age, Intellectual Property (IP) is often a business's most valuable asset. From tech startups to established manufacturing firms, protecting your brand and innovations is not optionalâ€”it's survival.

Trademarks vs. Patents vs. Copyrights

Understanding the difference is key to filing the right protection:

  • Trademarks: Protect brand identifiers (logos, slogans).
  • Patents: Protect inventions and industrial processes.
  • Copyrights: Protect original literary and artistic works.

The Registration Process

Registration with KIPI (Kenya Industrial Property Institute) or the Copyright Board is the only way to get statutory protection. Unregistered trademarks can only rely on the common law of "passing off," which is far harder to prove in court.

Enforcement

Having the certificate is step one; monitoring the market for infringers is step two. We help clients setup robust IP monitoring and take swift legal action through Cease & Desist orders when violations occur.
